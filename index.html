Beginning Roots

The beginning of my journey began in 2019 where I discoverd the realm of Data Analytics and Data Visualization. I quickly became fascinated with the idea of using data to build and create meaningful visualizations. I always enjoyed creating, building, and drawing things for a variety of projects whether it be personal or school-related growing up. Ironically, I did not enjoy math growing up. At all. So, I was hesitant to pursue anything that involved math. However, with time, I learned to tolerate math (and even enjoy it at times) through my interests in Data Analytics and Information Design.

I enjoy the process of designing, building, and understanding visualizations with data. I love that I can challenge myself when working with data sets because there is always something new to explore and probe. I rarely find myself running out of ideas when looking at data because there is so much one can do with it! When I'm not working with numbers, I enjoy spending my time reading, running, and finding inspiration for other creative activities.

Through my passions and interests I also hope to inspire and build memorable experiences/relationships for others as I am always ideating new ways to create and design things to make positive long lasting contributions.
